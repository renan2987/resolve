About Resolve

We got tired of pretty websites that did nothing.

So we built an agency that measures success in leads and revenue — not likes and awards.

Our story

Founded in New York on a simple frustration.

Too many businesses were paying good money for websites that looked fine and generated nothing. A logo here, a template there, and a monthly invoice for "marketing" nobody could explain.

Resolve started to fix that. We pair genuinely great website design and development with digital marketing that's accountable to real numbers. Every project has a target, every month has a report, and every decision is judged by whether it grows your business.

Today we're a compact, senior team serving restaurants, home-services, retailers, and local brands across NYC and beyond — the businesses that keep the city running.

Our mission

To make world-class web design and marketing accessible to the businesses that keep New York running.
